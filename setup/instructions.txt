Node.js
----------
install node
test it out with "node /bin/whatever/your/path/is.js" and verify
configure apache2 to proxy stuff over to node's ip/port
	ensure that the mod_proxy and mod_proxy_html (and a few other) moduels are installed into apache
	enable them via a2enmod
	put this line in /etc/apache2/apache2.conf:
		"ProxyPass /api http://localhost:8000" (or whatever your host/port is)
